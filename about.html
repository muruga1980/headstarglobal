Our Story

A Trading House Built on Trust & Consistency

Head Star Global is an ISO 9001:2015 certified leader in agro trading and export, established by a dedicated team of seasoned professionals with a wealth of experience in the retail and corporate sectors worldwide.

Our founders' extensive backgrounds encompass marketing, international trade, supply chain management and financial consulting — reflecting our commitment to holistic growth. Today, we operate a diversified trading business spanning agro products, domestic distribution, merchant trading and retail, with a registered office in Navi Mumbai, India and a trading network reaching markets across India and abroad.
