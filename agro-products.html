Rice

A Wide Range of High-Quality Rice

Our Basmati Rice range includes 1121 Basmati Steam, Pusa DB Basmati Steam, Sharbathi Basmati Steam, PR 11 Basmati Steam and Sugandha Basmati Steam — processed on steaming which ensures the rice is long, clean and takes very little time to cook.

We also offer Non-Basmati Rice acclaimed for its excellent taste and fresh aroma, including Sona Masuri, IR64, Parboiled, Matta and Long Grain Rice — lightweight, easy to digest and with a longer shelf life.

Sugar

Sourced From the Finest Cane Fields

Our sugar is sourced from the finest sugar cane fields, and we work with trusted suppliers to ensure every batch meets our high standards for quality and purity — available in bulk containers, bags, and customized packaging.

We understand that global demand for sugar is on the rise, and we're well-positioned to meet this demand with large volumes of high-quality sugar for food manufacturers, distributors and retailers alike.
