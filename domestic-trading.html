Domestic Trading

Your Reliable Partner in Domestic Agro Trading

Head Star Global Pvt Ltd plays a significant role in domestic trading across India, offering a variety of essential commodities to cater to the diverse needs of the local market. Our operations ensure seamless supply chain management from procurement to delivery, emphasizing quality, reliability and customer satisfaction.

How We Operate

Built on Strategic Foundations

Retail Packing Centeradd

Located in Maharashtra, our state-of-the-art retail packing center specializes in packaging high-quality products to meet safety and hygiene standards — handling bulk and retail packing efficiently, with customized sizes for small retail packs, family packs and wholesale bulk orders.

Strategic Partnership with NACOFadd

Our partnership with the National Agricultural Cooperative Marketing Federation of India (NACOF) strengthens our supply chain and broadens our reach in the domestic market — supporting farmers, cooperatives and local businesses through a mutually beneficial relationship.

Extensive Distribution Network Across Indiaadd

Headquartered in Maharashtra, we've established a robust distribution network reaching all corners of the country — including both southern and northern markets — minimizing delivery times and logistical costs even in remote areas.
