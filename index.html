Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What does Head Star Global trade in? add

We trade a diversified range of agricultural commodities including food grains, pulses & lentils, edible oils, spices, oil seeds, oil meal extractions, fruits & vegetables and other agro-products, serving both domestic and international markets.

Is Head Star Global a certified trading company? add

Yes. We are an ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 22000:2018 certified organization, and hold additional recognitions including FSSAI, APEDA, UKAS, DGFT, Halal Committee, Star Export House, Saudi FDA, MSME and Spices Board certifications.

Do you handle both domestic and export trading? add

Yes. Our domestic trading division serves markets across India through an extensive distribution network, while our merchant trading division manages global export and import logistics.

How can I request a quote or place an order? add

Simply fill out the enquiry form on our Contact Us page or message us directly on WhatsApp — our trade desk typically responds within one business day.

Where is Head Star Global registered? add

Our registered office is located in Navi Mumbai, Maharashtra, India, with an established trading network reaching markets across India and abroad.
