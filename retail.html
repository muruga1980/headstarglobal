Retail Distribution

Trusted Commodities, Closer to You

Headstar Retail Distribution is the logistics and sourcing backbone of Head Star Global Pvt Ltd, designed to ensure the timely movement of essential goods to India's villages and semi-urban markets.

With an expanding network of regional hubs, digital tracking systems and strong sourcing partnerships, Headstar Retail Distribution delivers reliability, affordability and uninterrupted availability of FMCG and grocery products across the Headstar retail network — while opening the door for local entrepreneurs to partner with us through franchising.

How We Operate

Built for Last-Mile Reliability

Regional Distribution Hubsadd

A growing network of regional hubs positioned close to demand centres, enabling faster replenishment cycles and reduced transit times to villages and semi-urban markets across India.

Centralized Sourcingadd

A single, consolidated sourcing operation for FMCG and grocery categories gives our retail partners consistent pricing, fewer stock-outs and simplified vendor management.

Private Label Supplyadd

We support private label programs for retail partners who want their own branded packaging on staples and grocery essentials, backed by our sourcing and quality infrastructure.

Temperature & Hygiene Controladd

Storage and transport handling follows strict temperature and hygiene protocols, protecting product quality from our hubs all the way to the retail shelf.

Transparent & Tech-Driven Operationsadd

Digital tracking systems give partners real-time visibility into stock movement and delivery status, replacing guesswork with data-driven replenishment decisions.
